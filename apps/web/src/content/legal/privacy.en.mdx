## 2. Data we collect

We collect only the data strictly necessary for each purpose. Breakdown per surface:

- **User account (when you sign up):** email, password (stored as a bcrypt hash by Supabase Auth), creation date, last-login date, preferred locale.
- **Newsletter:** email, subscription date, accepted consent-text version, IP + user-agent at sign-up time (LGPD Art. 9 accountability).

- **Cookies and local storage:** see Section 8.
- **Error telemetry (Sentry):** stack traces, route URL, user-agent, anonymous metadata. PII is filtered by a field scrubber before transmission.

**We do NOT collect:** CPF, RG, banking data, GPS location, biometrics, data from minors under 18 (see Section 10).

### Balancing test — Sentry (legitimate interest, errors only)

The **always-on** Sentry layer captures **application errors only** (stack traces + route metadata) on the basis of **legitimate interest (LGPD Art. 7 VIII)**. Balancing-test outcome:

- **Legitimate purpose:** ensure service stability, security, and availability — a legitimate expectation of any user.
- **Necessity:** without error telemetry, critical bugs (data loss, checkout failures, login failures) may go unnoticed for days, causing direct harm to the data subject.
- **Minimization applied:** PII (email, phone, CPF) is filtered before transmission by a dedicated scrubber applied to breadcrumbs, messages, headers, and HTTP request bodies. Performance sampling (`tracesSampleRate`) and session replay (`replaysSessionSampleRate` / `replaysOnErrorSampleRate`) are set to **zero** in this layer.
- **Protection:** 30-day retention; access restricted to authenticated administrators with 2FA.
- **Right to object:** you can object to this processing at any time via privacidade@bythiagofigueiredo.com (see Section 7). To opt out before transmission you may block third-party JavaScript or request account deletion (Section 7).

### Additional layer — Sentry Browser Tracing + Session Replay (analytics consent)

When — and **only when** — you accept the **analytics** cookies in the consent banner, two additional Sentry components become active:

- **Browser Tracing:** navigation-performance sampling (route load times, API request timings).
- **Session Replay:** session reconstruction with **all input boxes masked (`maskAllInputs: true`)** — what you type and passwords are never captured. Visible text on the page and images are recorded for visual reproduction of bugs.

These two components are attached dynamically upon granting analytics consent and **stopped at runtime** when consent is withdrawn. The legal basis is **consent (LGPD Art. 7 I / GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))**, and unlike the error layer, we **do not** rely on legitimate interest for tracing/replay.

We do NOT rely on Art. 7 IX (which does not exist under the Brazilian LGPD — the list ends at X).

### How to exercise your rights

Self-service tools available after login:

- **Access + portability:** `/account/export` — generates a full JSON package in schema **v1**, delivered via signed link (7-day validity).
- **Deletion:** `/account/delete` — 3-phase flow with email confirmation, immediate anonymization, **15-day grace period** to cancel, and permanent deletion at phase 3 (D+15).
- **Consents + cookies:** `/account/settings/privacy` — history and granular revocation.

For other rights, email **privacidade@bythiagofigueiredo.com** — we respond within **15 days** (LGPD Art. 19 §1).

### Multi-site scope of consent withdrawal

The platform operates as a **multi-site network** (the bythiagofigueiredo network), in which each domain — `bythiagofigueiredo.com` and sister sites that share the same infrastructure — records consents **independently, per site (`site_id`)**. This means that **withdrawing consent on one domain does NOT automatically cascade to the other sites in the network**: if you accepted analytics cookies on two distinct sites and later withdrew on one of them, the consent on the other site remains active until you explicitly withdraw it on that domain.

To manage consents across all sites you have visited in a consolidated view, go to **[/account/settings/privacy](/account/settings/privacy)** while authenticated — that screen lists all consents on your account across sites, with per-row withdrawal. Alternatively, you can visit the cookie banner on each domain.

## 9. Security

We apply technical and organizational measures proportional to risk:

- **TLS 1.3** on all connections
- **Row-Level Security (RLS)** on Supabase to isolate data between users
- **Bcrypt-hashed passwords** (never stored in plaintext)
- **Immutable audit logs** for administrative actions
- **Sentry with PII scrubber** — email/password fields are filtered before sending events
- **Rate limiting** on sensitive endpoints (verify-password, request-deletion, request-export)
- **Daily encrypted backup**, retained for 30 days

In case of a **security incident** involving personal data exposure, we will notify ANPD and affected data subjects within the LGPD Art. 48 timeframes.

## 10. Minors under 18 (LGPD Art. 14)

This site is **not directed at minors under 18** — the content, formats, and channels target an adult audience. However, we acknowledge that minors may access or use the site, and this section describes our handling of such cases under **LGPD Art. 14**.

### Legal basis and parental consent (LGPD Art. 14 §1)

When a data subject is identified as a **child (under 13)** or **adolescent (13 to 17)**, processing of their personal data may occur only:

- **For children (Art. 14 §1):** with **specific and prominent consent given by at least one parent or legal guardian**;
- **For adolescents:** always observing the **best interest** of the data subject, and — where consent is the applicable legal basis — with consent from the legal guardian when required by the prevailing ANPD interpretation.

### No age gate at collection (full disclosure)

We do not use an automated age gate at signup or on public forms. This is a **conscious decision** that, while it reduces friction, may result in minor registrations without prior parental-consent validation. We mitigate that risk with the mechanisms below.

### Channel for legal guardians

Parents and legal guardians who identify that a minor under their care has registered or provided data on this site may send a request to **privacidade@bythiagofigueiredo.com** with the subject "LGPD Art. 14 — minor". On that channel we accept:

- Requests for **immediate deletion** of the minor's data;
- Requests for **access** to the data collected;
- Requests for **correction** or **opposition** to processing.

### Response and deletion SLA (Art. 14)

Upon being notified by a legal guardian that a minor has registered data **without parental consent**, we commit to **deleting the data within 15 calendar days** counted from notification, confirm the deletion by email to the guardian, and record the operation in our audit log for accountability.

### Data treated as sensitive

Minors' data receives **the same technical protection level** applied to all data subjects (TLS 1.3, RLS, encrypted backups, Sentry minimization) — and is **not used for secondary purposes** (marketing, personalization, profiling) under any circumstance.

## 12. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

Thiago Figueiredo operates this site as a **natural person** (solo operator). Under **ANPD Resolution CD/ANPD No. 2 of January 27, 2022** (LGPD Application Regulation for Small-Sized Processing Agents), **small-sized agents are exempt from the mandatory appointment of a formal DPO** (Art. 11 of the Resolution).

In addition, under **GDPR Art. 37(1)**, the obligation to appoint a DPO applies only to: public authorities, entities whose core activities require regular and systematic large-scale monitoring, or those processing special categories at large scale — none of which applies here.

**EU representative (GDPR Art. 27):** Art. 27 of the GDPR requires controllers outside the EU to designate a written representative inside the EU when they offer goods/services to EU residents or monitor their behaviour in a substantial way. EU-originating traffic to this site is currently marginal (under 1% of monthly total), fitting the Art. 27(2)(a) exception — "occasional processing, which does not include, on a large scale, processing of special categories". If the EU visitor volume becomes material and the exception no longer applies, we will formally designate an EU representative and update this section with the relevant contact. Until then, EU visitors may use the privacy channel below.

**Privacy contact channel (single point of contact):** **privacidade@bythiagofigueiredo.com**

All LGPD/GDPR-related communications must be addressed to this channel. We respond within **15 calendar days**.
